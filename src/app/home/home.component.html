About Us

At Sirika Scrap Wala, we specialize in facilitating the trading of scrap materials, providing a seamless platform for businesses
to buy and sell recyclable materials. With a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility, we strive to create
a more circular economy by efficiently managing scrap resources. Our main emphasis is on serving to multinational corporations
(MNCs), business process outsourcing (BPO) firms, banks, hospitals, industries, educational institutions, and the government sector.
Leveraging our extensive experience in waste management, we have cultivated a customer-centric business philosophy centered around
proactive and timely customer service, which forms the foundation of our company.
